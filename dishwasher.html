Looking into the Sink, 2022, aluminum sheets and pastel on paper, 20 x 26”

A bird's eye view of wet dishes was chosen to give the illusion that the viewer is staring down into a sink. Arranged according to different curved and straight sizes and shapes, the objects are set at different angles in juxtaposition to each other. A heightened sense of depth and volume are achieved by intentionally shading various objects darker or highlighting them with tones. I used aluminum sheets to imitate the sink’s countertop and to relate to an illusion of smooth, reflective surfaces. Touches of pink enliven the color scheme and help to unify the scene. .
