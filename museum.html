Museum of Fragility, 2025, wood, clay, lens and fabric, 13 x 22", Collaboration with Olivia Liu

Curating a museum through a prototype model using artworks from the RISD Museum. Museum of Fragility is not a physical place, instead it is a mental place to explores the fragility of the human mind. Inspired by the Kodak Camera and lenses, this piece uses the Concave lens to distort the view to communicate a distortion of relaity.
